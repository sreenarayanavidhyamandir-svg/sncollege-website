Institutional Profile

Sree Narayana College of Arts and Science, Kundalassery, Keralassery, Palakkad, is established with the noble vision of creating a prosperous, inclusive, and enlightened society through quality education and holistic student development.

The institution is deeply inspired and guided by the timeless philosophies and teachings of
👉 Sree Narayana Guru

His ideals of equality, social justice, and empowerment form the foundation of the college’s academic and developmental approach.

🌱 Commitment to Social Upliftment

The college gives special emphasis to:

Educational empowerment of students from weaker sections
Ensuring equal opportunities for all
Supporting the economic and social upliftment of learners
Building a value-based and inclusive academic environment
